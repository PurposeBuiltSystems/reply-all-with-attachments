Add-in: Reply All with Attachments

What it does

Adds a Reply All with Attachments button to the Outlook message ribbon. Open a received email, click the button, and a Reply All compose window opens with the original attachments included — restoring the attachments Outlook normally removes when you reply.

How to use it

  1. Open a received email that has attachments.
  2. On the ribbon, click Reply All with Attachments.
  3. A Reply All window opens with the attachments already attached. Edit and send as usual.

Troubleshooting

  • Button doesn't appear: make sure you're viewing a received message (not a draft), and that your account is a Microsoft 365 / Exchange Online account. Very old Outlook builds may not be supported.
  • Some attachments didn't carry over: very large attachments can exceed Outlook's reply-form limit and are skipped; the add-in shows a notice when this happens.
  • Inline images: images embedded in the message body stay in the quoted reply automatically and are not added a second time.
