Privacy Policy

Add-in: Reply All with Attachments · Last updated: June 2026

Summary: this add-in does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. It has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party trackers.

What the add-in does

Reply All with Attachments adds a button to the Outlook message ribbon. When you click it on an open email, it reads that email's attachments and opens a Reply All compose window with those attachments re-attached. This restores attachments that Outlook normally removes when you reply.

Data we collect

None. The add-in does not collect, log, or retain any information about you, your email, your attachments, or your usage.

How your email content is handled

When you click the button, the add-in signs you in (Microsoft nested app authentication) and uses Microsoft Graph — Microsoft's own Microsoft 365 service — to create the Reply All draft and copy the original attachments onto it. Your message and attachments travel only between your mailbox and Microsoft Graph; they stay entirely within the Microsoft 365 boundary.

There is no PurposeBuilt Systems server in this process, and no third party receives your data. The add-in connects directly to Microsoft Graph from your own client; we never see, receive, store, or proxy your email or attachments. The add-in accesses only the signed-in user's own mailbox (delegated permission) — never anyone else's.

Optional signature

If you choose to set a reply signature, it is saved only in your own Outlook roaming settings — a per-mailbox storage area inside Microsoft 365. PurposeBuilt Systems never receives, sees, or stores your signature; it stays in your own mailbox and is read back only on your own device to add it to your reply. This feature is entirely optional.

Hosting

The add-in's program files (a small amount of HTML, JavaScript, and icon images) are served as static files from GitHub Pages. This host only delivers those program files to Outlook; it does not, and technically cannot, receive your email content back. Standard web-server access logs kept by the hosting provider may record routine request metadata (such as the file requested), but never any email or attachment content.

Cookies and tracking

The add-in sets no cookies and uses no analytics or advertising technologies.

Permissions

The add-in uses the Outlook read item permission (to read the open message and open the reply) and the Microsoft Graph Mail.ReadWrite delegated permission (to create the reply and copy the attachments). "Delegated" means the add-in acts only as the signed-in user and can reach only that user's own mailbox — never other people's mail. No application-level or mailbox-wide access is requested.
